About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, the challenges become increasingly difficult, testing your skills and strategy.

Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and unlock new abilities to enhance your survival chances. With its addictive gameplay and progressive difficulty, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face wave after wave of unique monsters with increasing difficulty. How long can you survive?

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and discover powerful combinations to enhance your survival strategy.

Various Enemies

Battle against a diverse array of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Power-ups & Items

Collect various power-ups and items throughout your journey to gain advantages against the monster hordes.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrades when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible!
